PBI Wallpaper Colour Shifter

Quickly swap the colour palette of your Power BI wallpaper SVG files. Upload an SVG, enter your desired hex colour codes (or click the swatch to pick a colour), and press Shift to generate a recoloured version. The tool replaces specific colours within the SVG source — primary (#2C5234), secondary (#f5f5f5), and tertiary (#e6e6e6) — with your custom values.
