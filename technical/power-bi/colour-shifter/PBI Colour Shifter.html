PBI Wallpaper Colour Shifter

Quickly swap the colour palette of your Power BI wallpaper SVG files. Upload an SVG, choose your colours from the brand palette (or type a hex code), and press Shift to generate a recoloured version. The tool replaces specific colours within the SVG source — primary (#2C5234), secondary (#f5f5f5), and tertiary (#e6e6e6) — with your custom values.
